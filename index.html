Public Hearing:

Agenda item number

  1. Read the title of the item:
  2. Ask for a motion to open the public hearing.
    • Motion
    • Second
    • Discussion
    • Vote
  3. Ask staff to provide background information.
  4. Invite applicant to step forward to address commission.
  5. Ask if there are any citizens who wish to be heard either for or against the requested action. Once at the podium, ask them to begin by stating their name and address for the record.
  6. Ask for a motion to close the public hearing.
    • Motion
    • Second
    • Discussion
    • Vote
  7. Ask commissioners for a motion on the item. Motioner must state the entire motion with any recommended conditions set forth by staff or changes to said conditions.
    • Motion
    • Second
  8. Open the floor to discussion by commission members. Once all members have completed their debate, the chair asks members if they are ready for the question or vote.
  9. Ask for a vote on the motion. The chair may request an individual voice vote to ensure an accurate record.
  10. Announce the outcome of the vote.
    • Motion passes OR
    • Motion fails
  11. Announce the next item of business.
